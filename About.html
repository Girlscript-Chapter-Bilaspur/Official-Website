WHO WE ARE ?

We are considered as a chapter of Girlscript India foundation, which is a section-8 non profit company, registered under the government of India on the 2nd June, 2017. Our aim is to spread awareness about technology and to provide a platform for everyone to generate an identity. With some innovative ideas our core team has 1 Chapter Leader with 1 community manager, 6 leads, 8 coordinators and 23+ members. We are looking forward to an awesome experience in learning new skills and building a better community.
